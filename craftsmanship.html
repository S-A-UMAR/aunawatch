The Art of Creation

Each AUNA watch is a masterpiece born from decades of expertise, refined techniques, and an obsessive attention to detail. We don't just make watches—we craft heirlooms.

The Watchmaking Process

01

Design & Engineering

Every watch begins as a vision. Our designers sketch, refine, and engineer each timepiece with CAD precision, ensuring form and function achieve perfect harmony.

02

Material Selection

We source only the finest materials from certified suppliers. Sapphire crystal, stainless steel, and precious metals are hand-selected for durability and beauty.

03

Case Crafting

Each case is machined from a solid block of metal. Hours of CNC work are followed by hand-finishing to achieve the signature AUNA polish and brushing.

04

Movement Assembly

Our master watchmakers assemble the movement by hand. Every gear, jewel, and spring is placed with millimeter precision. No shortcuts. No exceptions.

05

Dial Creation

The dial is hand-painted and applied with precision. Each marker, text, and detail is positioned to exact specifications using traditional techniques.

06

Quality Control

Every watch undergoes rigorous testing—accuracy, water resistance, durability. Only timepieces that exceed our standards leave the workshop.
