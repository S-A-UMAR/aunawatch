Inside

A mechanism built for a lifetime.

The calibre inside AUNA No. 1 winds itself from the motion of your wrist. No battery, no charging cable — just pure mechanical excellence. Every movement is a work of art, designed and assembled with meticulous attention to detail.

Every movement is regulated by hand over six days before it's allowed to leave the workshop, and finished with a côtes de Genève pattern visible through the exhibition caseback. We stand behind our craftsmanship with a two-year warranty.

Materials that age with grace.

The case is cold-forged from a single billet of 316L stainless steel, hand-brushed along its top face and polished along the bevel — the kind of contrast you feel before you see.

Choose the steel bracelet to match how you actually wear a watch, not how it photographs. Our straps are interchangeable, so you can change your look in seconds.
