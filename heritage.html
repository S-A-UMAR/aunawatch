The AUNA Heritage

For over two decades, AUNA has been dedicated to creating timepieces that transcend trends. Our watches are more than instruments—they're heirlooms crafted with precision, passion, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

2004

The Foundation

AUNA was founded on a simple principle: to create watches that honor the craft of traditional horology while embracing modern design.

2008

First Collection

The debut collection introduced AUNA's signature design language—clean, elegant, and timeless. Within the first year, we became recognized across Europe.

2014

Swiss Partnership

We partnered with master watchmakers in Geneva to elevate our movement precision, achieving chronometer standards across all collections.

2020

Sustainable Innovation

Introduced our sustainable sourcing initiative, ensuring every component meets the highest environmental and ethical standards.

2024

Global Icon

AUNA watches are now recognized worldwide as symbols of timeless elegance and uncompromising craftsmanship. Worn by artists, architects, and makers.

Our Values

⚒️

Craftsmanship

Every AUNA watch is assembled by hand with precision and care. We believe in the meticulous attention to detail that defines true luxury.

🌍

Responsibility

We source materials ethically and manufacture sustainably. Our watches honor both artistry and planet.

♾️

Timelessness

Design trends fade. AUNA watches are created to last generations, becoming heirlooms passed down with pride.

Our Vision for Tomorrow

At AUNA, we believe the future of watchmaking lies in honoring the past while innovating boldly. We're not chasing the newest technology—we're perfecting the timeless art of horology.

We envision a world where quality matters more than quantity, where craftsmanship is celebrated, and where every person can own a watch that tells their story. A watch that becomes part of their identity, their legacy.
