Crafting Time, Honoring Tradition

Founded in 2024, AUNA is dedicated to the art of watchmaking. We believe that a watch is more than just a tool for telling time — it's a companion for life's moments, big and small.

Every AUNA timepiece is meticulously crafted with attention to detail, using only the finest materials. From the cold-forged steel case to the hand-finished movement, each component is a testament to our commitment to quality.

Our Values

Quality First

We never compromise on quality. Every watch is tested rigorously to ensure it meets our exacting standards before it leaves our workshop.

Timeless Design

We create watches that look as good today as they will in 50 years. Simple, elegant, and built to withstand passing trends.

Sustainability
